Wireless Charging System for Static and Dynamic EVs

Objective:

Develop a Wireless Charging System for static and dynamic Electric Vehicles (EVs) to enhance convenience and efficiency in EV charging infrastructure, addressing both stationary and on-the-move charging needs.

Features:

Static Charging: Efficient wireless charging for parked EVs.
Dynamic Charging: Seamless charging while the EV is in motion.
Automated Alignment: Autonomous alignment for precise charging connection.

Pros:

Convenience: Eliminates physical plugs and cords.
Efficiency: Reduces charging downtime with dynamic charging.
Flexibility: Adapts to various EV models and charging scenarios.
Reduced Maintenance: Minimizes wear and tear associated with physical connectors

Cons:

Initial Cost: Higher setup cost compared to traditional charging stations.
Power Limitations: Charging speed may be limited by available power.

Use Cases:

Urban Environments: Enhances EV charging infrastructure in cities.
Public Transport: Facilitates dynamic charging for electric buses.
Fleet Management: Streamlines charging for shared EV fleets.
